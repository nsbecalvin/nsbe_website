Why Join NSBE?

Professional Networking

Connect with industry professionals, alumni, and fellow students. Build relationships that will advance your career and open doors to internship and job opportunities.

Academic Excellence

Access tutoring, study groups, and academic resources tailored for engineering students. Our community supports your academic journey from freshman to senior year.

Leadership Development

Develop essential leadership skills through committee work, executive positions, and organizing events. Gain experience that sets you apart in the job market.

Member Opportunities

Career Development

Participate in workshops, career fairs, and mentorship programs. Get guidance on resume building, interview skills, and career planning from experienced professionals.

Community Engagement

Make a positive impact through outreach programs, community service, and STEM education initiatives. Inspire the next generation of Black engineers and scientists.

Social Events

Build lasting friendships through social gatherings, cultural celebrations, and networking events. Join a supportive community that celebrates diversity and achievement.

NSBE Membership Benefits

National Connection

Join over 24,000 members across 600+ chapters. Access national conferences, competitions, and resources that enhance your professional development.

Scholarship Opportunities

Apply for NSBE-specific scholarships and awards. Our members have access to exclusive funding opportunities to support their education.

Industry Partnerships

Connect with top engineering firms and corporations through NSBE's corporate partnerships. Many companies actively recruit from our membership.

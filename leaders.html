Our Leadership Team

Marcus Johnson

President

Marcus is a senior Computer Engineering student with a passion for creating inclusive spaces in tech. He has interned at Microsoft and Google, and leads our chapter with a vision for increasing representation in STEM fields. Under his leadership, our membership has grown by 40%.

Vice President

Jasmine is a Mechanical Engineering major with minors in Business and Leadership. She coordinates all chapter events and manages our corporate partnerships. Her efforts have secured over $50,000 in sponsorship funding for our programs this year.

The Executive Board

Our organization exists to live out the NSBE Mission: "to increase the number of culturally responsible Black Engineers who excel academically, succeed professionally and positively impact the community."

Our leadership team puts on numerous events, programs, and initiatives to serve Calvin University and the Greater Grand Rapids community, creating a supportive environment for Black engineers to thrive.

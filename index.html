Welcome to Our NSBE Chapter

We are a dedicated chapter of the National Society of Black Engineers committed to increasing the number of culturally responsible Black engineers who excel academically, succeed professionally, and positively impact the community. At the Calvin University Society of Black Engineers, we are committed to fostering an inclusive and supportive environment for Black engineers.​ As a dynamic community within Calvin, we offer: Networking and Connections: Engage with fellow students, alumni, and industry professionals to build valuable relationships and expand your network. Career Development: Access workshops, mentorship programs, and career fairs tailored to enhance your skills and advance your career. Academic Support: Benefit from tutoring, study groups, and resources designed to help you excel in your engineering studies. Community Engagement: Participate in meaningful outreach programs and community service initiatives that make a positive impact. Social and Cultural Activities: Enjoy events and gatherings that celebrate our diverse backgrounds and foster a sense of belonging. Explore our website to find information on upcoming events, membership opportunities, and how you can get involved. We are excited to have you join our community.​ Welcome to GTSBE, where your success and growth are our top priorities.

About NSBE

The National Society of Black Engineers (NSBE) is one of the largest student-governed organizations based in the United States. NSBE has more than 600 chapters and nearly 24,000 active members in the U.S. and abroad.
